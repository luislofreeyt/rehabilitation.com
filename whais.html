What is Rehabilitation?

Rehabilitation is a set of interventions designed to optimize functioning and reduce disability in individuals with health conditions in interaction with their environment.

Physical Therapy

Helps restore movement and function after injury or illness.

Speech Therapy

Improves communication skills for individuals with language challenges.

Occupational Therapy

Supports daily living activities and enhances life skills.
