Types of Rehabilitation

Physical Rehabilitation

Focuses on restoring movement and physical function after injury or surgery.

Speech and Language Therapy

Helps improve communication and swallowing disorders in patients of all ages.

Occupational Therapy

Assists patients in regaining daily life skills and independence at home or work.

Cognitive Rehabilitation

Targets memory, attention, and problem-solving after brain injuries or strokes.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Designed to help people recover and improve their heart health after a cardiac event.

Substance Abuse Rehabilitation

Focuses on recovery and reintegration for individuals affected by drug or alcohol use.
